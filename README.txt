SPEW
An entry for the Spring 2014 UCSD VGDC Game Jam. Created in the Unity game engine.

Description
Spew! You are a little orange dinosaur who cannot hold his stomach. You can't help but spew lava everywhere! Complete your quest to become kawaii!


Controls
W,A,S,D - Movement
Spacebar - Spew a ball of lava.
